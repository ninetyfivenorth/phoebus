What are Add-ons? Add-ons let you customize Pale Moon with extra functionality or a different look. There are several types of add-ons. Extensions expand the capabilities of the browser, themes allow you to personalize Pale Moon's visual aesthetic, and plugins further extend the browser with specialized external components such as the Adobe Flash Player.

You can take advantage of Pale Moon's exceptional extensibility by installing add-ons from your choice of the following repository sites:

Pale Moon Add-ons Site

Browse add-ons specifically for Pale Moon and cross-check for known compatible Firefox add-ons

Mozilla Add-ons Site

Mozilla offers add-ons primarily for the Firefox browser which may be compatible with Pale Moon
